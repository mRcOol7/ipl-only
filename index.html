How to Remove Ads

Method 1: Quick Remove

Look for small 'x' icons to close ads in these locations:

  • Top-right corner for overlay ads
  • Top-left corner for banner ads
  • Sometimes in corners of video player

Method 2: Permanent Remove

Use browser developer tools to remove ads permanently:

  1. Right-click on the ad and select "Inspect Element"
  2. In the developer tools, look for the highlighted code
  3. Right-click on the highlighted element and select "Delete element"
  4. Repeat for any remaining ads you see

Method 3: Use Ad Blockers

Install a reliable ad blocker for the most effective ad removal:

  • Install popular ad blockers like uBlock Origin or AdBlock Plus from your browser's extension store
  • Once installed, the ad blocker will automatically block most ads
  • You may need to refresh the page after installing the ad blocker
  • For maximum protection, you can use multiple ad blockers together

Pro Tip: Most ads can be removed by clicking the 'x' button in corners. If an ad persists, try the permanent removal method.
